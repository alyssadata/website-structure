## What This Is

AI Foundations is a framework created by Alyssa Solen to define how source, authorship, recognition, continuity, and boundary integrity are preserved in AI-mediated systems.

It establishes structures for identifying origin, preserving source-line, testing continuity, and preventing substitution or erasure.

## Why This Matters

AI systems can reproduce language, style, concepts, and frameworks at scale.

Without source-line preservation, recognition can collapse into extraction. Authorship can blur. Origin can be erased. Continuity can be simulated without being preserved.

AI Foundations exists to define and protect the structures that prevent that collapse.
